Each state is its own file in this folder, plus sprite.svg holding all of them as <symbol>s. Drop the sprite in your page and pull any part with <use href="#btn-a-pressed"/>, or swap the href on press. The thumbstick ships as two files — pivot and stick — so you can move the stick alone; its centre sits at translate(32,32) on the pivot canvas. Everything is plain SVG: no fonts to load, no raster, recolour by editing the gradient stops.
